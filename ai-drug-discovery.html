Why Choose Evionex for Drug Discovery?

Drug discovery is a time-intensive process with high failure rates, often due to poor target selection and limited understanding of biological mechanisms. Evionex uses AI to streamline this process, identifying high-potential targets and reducing the risk of late-stage failures.

We go beyond traditional drug discovery by integrating multi-omics data, using deep learning to predict drug-target interactions, and simulating biological responses. Our approach ensures faster, more accurate target identification and prioritization.

Popular Techniques We Cover

Target Prediction with Deep Learning

Using neural networks to predict drug targets based on multi-omics data.

Common Pitfall: Overreliance on single-omics data can miss critical interactions.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We integrate genomics, proteomics, and metabolomics data to train our models, ensuring a holistic view of biological systems.

Virtual Screening

Screening compound libraries against predicted targets.

Common Pitfall: High false-positive rates due to simplistic scoring functions.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We use ensemble machine learning models to improve scoring accuracy and validate hits with molecular dynamics simulations.

ADMET Prediction

Predicting Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, Excretion, and Toxicity profiles.

Common Pitfall: Models often fail to account for biological variability.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We incorporate patient-specific multi-omics data to personalize ADMET predictions and assess off-target effects.
