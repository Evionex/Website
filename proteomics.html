Proteomics Analysis

Advanced solutions for studying protein expression and interactions.

Why Choose Evionex for Proteomics?

Proteomics involves intricate workflows, from sample preparation to data interpretation, with challenges like protein quantification accuracy and post-translational modification (PTM) detection. Evionex provides expert analysis to navigate these complexities, ensuring reliable and reproducible results.

We go beyond standard proteomics by integrating with transcriptomics and metabolomics data, using AI to predict protein interactions, and providing detailed PTM profiling. Our custom pipelines and advanced visualization tools help you uncover deeper biological insights.

Popular Techniques We Cover

Mass Spectrometry (MS)-based Proteomics

High-throughput identification and quantification of proteins.

Common Pitfall: Incomplete peptide identification due to database limitations.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We use de novo sequencing and custom databases to identify novel peptides, followed by pathway analysis to contextualize findings.

Label-Free Quantification (LFQ)

Quantify proteins without isotopic labeling.

Common Pitfall: Variability in sample preparation can skew quantification results.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We implement rigorous quality control and normalization, integrating with transcriptomics to validate protein expression changes.

Tandem Mass Tag (TMT) Labeling

Multiplexed quantification of proteins across multiple samples.

Common Pitfall: Ratio compression can underestimate fold changes.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We apply correction algorithms and cross-validate with LFQ to ensure accurate quantification.

And much more! We offer a wide range of proteomics techniques tailored to your research needs.
