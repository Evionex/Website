Data Integration

Seamlessly integrate multi-omics data for holistic analysis.

Why Choose Evionex for Data Integration?

Integrating multi-omics data is challenging due to differences in data types, scales, and biological contexts. Evionex provides expert solutions to harmonize your data, ensuring a comprehensive view of biological systems.

We go beyond standard integration by using AI-driven approaches to identify cross-omics interactions, applying advanced normalization techniques, and providing custom visualizations to interpret integrated data. Our methods help you uncover insights that single-omics analyses miss.

Popular Techniques We Cover

Canonical Correlation Analysis (CCA)

Identifying correlations between different omics layers.

Common Pitfall: Ignores non-linear relationships between datasets.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We use non-linear CCA and machine learning to capture complex interactions across omics layers.

Multi-Omics Factor Analysis (MOFA)

Uncovering latent factors driving variation across omics datasets.

Common Pitfall: Limited interpretability of latent factors.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We combine MOFA with pathway analysis to interpret factors in a biological context.

Network-Based Integration

Constructing biological networks to integrate multi-omics data.

Common Pitfall: Static networks miss dynamic biological changes.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We build dynamic networks using time-series data and AI to predict temporal interactions.

And much more! We offer a wide range of data integration techniques tailored to your research needs.
