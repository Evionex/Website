Genomics and Transcriptomics Analysis

Comprehensive solutions for genetic and transcriptional data analysis.

Why Choose Evionex for Genomics and Transcriptomics?

Genomics and transcriptomics are complex fields requiring precise data handling, advanced computational tools, and deep biological expertise. At Evionex, we simplify this complexity by providing end-to-end analysis, from raw data processing to actionable insights. Our team ensures high-quality results, addressing challenges like data noise, batch effects, and biological variability.

We go beyond typical analysis by integrating multi-omics data, applying AI-driven models to uncover hidden patterns, and providing custom visualizations and reports tailored to your research needs. Our expertise ensures you get more than just standard results—you get insights that drive innovation.

Popular Techniques We Cover

RNA-seq

RNA sequencing to analyze gene expression and differential expression.

Common Pitfall: Many analyses fail to account for batch effects, leading to skewed differential expression results.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We apply advanced normalization techniques and use machine learning to identify and correct batch effects, followed by pathway enrichment analysis to reveal biological significance.

Spatial Transcriptomics

Mapping gene expression in spatial contexts to understand tissue organization.

Common Pitfall: Spatial data often suffers from low resolution, missing subtle expression patterns.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We use deconvolution algorithms to enhance resolution and integrate with single-cell RNA-seq data to provide a more detailed spatial map.

Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS)

Comprehensive sequencing of the entire genome to identify variants.

Common Pitfall: Standard pipelines often miss structural variants or rare mutations due to alignment issues.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We use long-read sequencing data integration and custom variant calling pipelines to detect structural variants and rare mutations with higher accuracy.

Whole Exome Sequencing (WES)

Targeted sequencing of exonic regions to identify coding variants.

Common Pitfall: Coverage biases can lead to missed variants in low-coverage regions.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We implement coverage correction algorithms and cross-validate with WGS data to ensure comprehensive variant detection.

ATAC-seq

Assay for Transposase-Accessible Chromatin sequencing to study chromatin accessibility.

Common Pitfall: Misinterpretation of open chromatin regions due to sequencing depth inconsistencies.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We perform deep integrative analysis with transcriptomics data to correlate chromatin accessibility with gene expression changes.

ChIP-seq

Chromatin Immunoprecipitation sequencing to study protein-DNA interactions.

Common Pitfall: High background noise can obscure true binding sites.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We use advanced peak-calling algorithms and integrate with ATAC-seq data to validate binding sites and assess functional impact.

And much more! We offer a wide range of techniques tailored to your specific research needs.
