A centralized platform to streamline your lab’s workflow with AI-powered grant writing, virtual lab notebook, and resource booking.

Why Choose EviNote?

EviNote simplifies lab management by addressing common challenges like scattered data, chaotic communication, and inefficient resource scheduling. Our platform ensures organization and accountability, saving you time for groundbreaking research.

With seamless integration of AI-driven tools and multi-functional features, EviNote goes beyond traditional lab notebooks, offering scientific illustration, financial transparency, and secure data storage for a holistic lab management experience.

Core Features of EviNote

AI-Powered Grant Writing

Challenge: Crafting compelling grant proposals is time-consuming and complex.

Solution: Our AI, trained for scientific grants, suggests funding opportunities, critiques drafts, and polishes proposals for maximum impact, streamlining the process.

Virtual Lab Notebook

Challenge: Scattered data and lost experiments hinder productivity.

Solution: Store and search all lab data securely with AI-powered note summarization, ensuring instant access to historical records anywhere, anytime.

Resource Booking

Challenge: Calendar conflicts and double-bookings disrupt lab operations.

Solution: Reserve shared instruments effortlessly with smart calendar integration and real-time notifications, ensuring smooth scheduling.

Scientific Illustrator

Challenge: Creating publication-quality figures is tedious and requires specialized skills.

Solution: Design professional-grade illustrations in minutes with a drag-and-drop interface and a library of scientifically accurate icons.

Financial Transparency

Challenge: Untracked expenses lead to financial mismanagement.

Solution: Log purchases with receipts for real-time oversight, ensuring accountability and eliminating surprises.

Team Collaboration

Challenge: Miscommunication and onboarding delays slow progress.

Solution: Streamline onboarding, task assignments, and team chat to keep everyone in sync and maintain continuity across generations.
