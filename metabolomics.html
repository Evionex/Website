Why Choose Evionex for Metabolomics?

Metabolomics requires sophisticated analytical techniques to identify and quantify metabolites accurately. Evionex provides expert support to handle challenges like metabolite identification, data normalization, and pathway mapping, ensuring reliable results.

We go beyond typical metabolomics by integrating with proteomics and transcriptomics data, using AI to predict metabolic interactions, and providing detailed pathway analysis. Our custom solutions help you uncover novel biomarkers and therapeutic targets.

Popular Techniques We Cover

LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry)

High-sensitivity detection of metabolites in complex samples.

Common Pitfall: Misidentification of metabolites due to database mismatches.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We use in-house spectral libraries and AI-driven annotation to improve identification accuracy, followed by pathway enrichment analysis.

GC-MS (Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry)

Analysis of volatile and semi-volatile metabolites.

Common Pitfall: Limited coverage of non-volatile metabolites.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We combine GC-MS with LC-MS to ensure comprehensive metabolite coverage and perform flux analysis to study metabolic dynamics.

NMR Spectroscopy

Non-destructive analysis of metabolite structures.

Common Pitfall: Low sensitivity for low-abundance metabolites.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We integrate NMR with MS data to enhance detection and perform metabolic modeling to predict pathway activity.

And much more! We offer a wide range of metabolomics techniques tailored to your research needs.
