Why Choose Evionex for Target Validation?

Target validation is critical to ensure the success of drug development, but traditional methods often fail to account for complex biological interactions. Evionex combines AI and multi-omics data to provide robust validation, reducing the risk of pursuing invalid targets.

We go beyond standard validation by integrating genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics data, using AI to predict target-disease associations, and performing functional assays to confirm biological relevance. Our approach ensures confidence in your target selection.

Popular Techniques We Cover

CRISPR Screening

Genome-wide knockout screens to validate target functionality.

Common Pitfall: Off-target effects can lead to false positives.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We integrate CRISPR data with transcriptomics to identify and correct off-target effects, ensuring accurate target validation.

RNA Interference (RNAi)

Gene silencing to assess target impact on disease phenotypes.

Common Pitfall: Incomplete knockdown can obscure true target effects.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We use multi-omics integration to confirm knockdown efficiency and assess downstream pathway effects.

AI-Driven Target Scoring

Ranking targets based on multi-omics data and disease relevance.

Common Pitfall: Overfitting models to limited datasets can lead to biased scoring.

Our Non-Typical Analysis: We use ensemble models and cross-validate with external datasets to ensure robust target prioritization.

And much more! We offer a wide range of target validation techniques tailored to your research needs.
